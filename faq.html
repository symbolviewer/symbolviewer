About this website


Q. What is Unicode?

A. Characters you see on mobile phones or computers are represented by numbers. Unicode gives a unique number to all characters of all languages.


Q. What is a Codepoint?

A. It is a unique number assigned to a character.


Q. What will I find on this website?

A. You can find any character of any language and copy it to clipboard. You will also see the number assigned to the character.


Q. Some characters are shown as empty squares. Why?

A. Because not all characters are supported on every platform.


Q. Is this website free?

A. Yes, it's totally free, but it contains some ads.


Q. I want only Emojis!

A. You can try emojipicker.com website.


Q. Do you have the list of composed characters, of more than one codepoint, on this website?

A. No.


Q. In what format do I see the character codes on this website?

A. UTF-32. Essentially all blocks of UTF-32 are covered on this site.


Q. Are unassigned codepoints also shown as blanks?

A. No. Unassigned codepoints are not shown on this site.


Q. I need UTF-16 or UTF-8 representations!

A. They may be supported in the future. This website is just born...
